Focus & Manufacturing

The institution focuses on manufacturing a variety of Ayurvedic formulations using carefully selected medicinal plants and natural ingredients. Their product range includes:

  • Tablets — standardised Ayurvedic formulations for systemic therapeutic use
  • Powders (Churna) — classical herbal powder preparations for digestive and general health
  • Herbal Oils — medicated oils for external application and therapeutic massage
  • Decoctions (Kadha) — traditional liquid preparations from boiled herbs
04

Quality & Standards

By combining traditional Ayurvedic knowledge with modern quality control and manufacturing practices, the organisation ensures the safety, purity, and effectiveness of its products. Key practices include:

  • Raw material selection — careful sourcing and authentication of medicinal plant material
  • Standardised production — consistent manufacturing methods aligned with classical Ayurvedic texts
  • Scientific quality testing — modern laboratory validation of purity, potency, and safety
  • Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) — adherence to regulatory standards for herbal medicines
05

Significance for Our Research

This field visit provided valuable firsthand exposure to the practical application of Ayurvedic principles in a professional pharmaceutical setting. Observing how traditional herbal knowledge is translated into standardised medicinal products offered important context for understanding the real-world relevance of the plants studied in this research.

The facility's emphasis on preserving classical Ayurvedic formulations while integrating modern quality control directly supports the goals of this project — demonstrating that plant-based medicines, including those with anticancer potential, can be developed responsibly and effectively for holistic healthcare.
